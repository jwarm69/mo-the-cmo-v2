## Optimal Posting Schedule

### Platform-Specific Best Times (EST - UF Campus Time)

#### TikTok
- **Peak Times**: 12:00 PM, 7:00 PM, 9:00 PM
- **Posting Frequency**: 1-2 times daily
- **Best Days**: Tuesday-Thursday (higher engagement)
- **Content Length**: 15-30 seconds optimal, max 60 seconds

#### Instagram Reels
- **Peak Times**: 11:00 AM, 2:00 PM, 5:00 PM
- **Posting Frequency**: 1 time daily
- **Best Days**: Wednesday-Friday
- **Content Length**: 15-30 seconds optimal

#### YouTube Shorts
- **Peak Times**: 3:00 PM, 8:00 PM
- **Posting Frequency**: 3-4 times per week
- **Best Days**: Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday
- **Content Length**: 30-60 seconds

**Theme**: Establishing core value propositions

### Week 2: Social Proof
**Theme**: Student testimonials and success stories
- **Focus**: Real user experiences and authenticity
- **Goal**: Build trust and credibility

### Week 3: Community Building
**Theme**: Campus culture and restaurant partnerships
- **Focus**: Local relevance and food quality
- **Goal**: Strengthen campus connection
- **KPIs**: Local engagement, restaurant mentions

### Week 4: Conversion Push
**Theme**: Call-to-action focused content
- **Focus**: Direct conversion and feature highlights
- **Goal**: Drive app downloads and first orders
- **KPIs**: Click-through rate, conversions, first orders

## Content Production Workflow

### Daily Routine
1. **Morning Check** (9:00 AM)
   - Review previous day's performance
   - Check trending topics/hashtags
   - Confirm day's content schedule

2. **Content Creation** (10:00 AM - 2:00 PM)
   - Film/photograph planned content
   - Edit and prepare for posting
   - Write captions and hashtags

3. **Afternoon Posting** (2:00 PM - 6:00 PM)
   - Schedule posts for optimal times
   - Monitor initial engagement
   - Respond to comments/messages

4. **Evening Review** (7:00 PM - 9:00 PM)
   - Analyze performance metrics
   - Engage with community
   - Plan next day's content

### Weekly Planning
- **Monday**: Plan week's content themes
- **Wednesday**: Mid-week performance review
- **Friday**: Week wrap-up and next week planning
- **Sunday**: Content batch creation day

### Monthly Review
- Analyze top-performing content
- Identify trending topics and opportunities  
- Adjust content pillar percentages based on performance
- Plan next month's seasonal content
