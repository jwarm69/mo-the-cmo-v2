# Bite Club Influencer Outreach & Ambassador Program Strategy

## Campus Micro-Influencer Identification & Outreach

### Target Influencer Profile

#### Primary Targets: Campus Micro-Influencers (1K-10K followers)
- **Demographics**: UF students aged 18-24
- **Content Focus**: Campus life, food, student budget tips, lifestyle
- **Engagement Rate**: 3%+ (more important than follower count)
- **Authenticity Markers**: Real student experiences, genuine personality, local relevance

#### Secondary Targets: Campus Nano-Influencers (500-5K followers)
- **Benefits**: Higher engagement rates, more affordable partnerships
- **Content Quality**: Often more authentic and relatable
- **Community Impact**: Strong local influence despite smaller reach

### Influencer Discovery Process

#### Manual Research Methods
1. **Hashtag Mining**
   - Search: #UF, #UniversityofFlorida, #UFStudents, #GainesvilleEats, #CollegeLife
   - Note: Students posting regularly about campus food, lifestyle, money-saving tips
   - Tool: Instagram/TikTok search functions, hashtag exploration

2. **Location-Based Discovery**
   - **Campus Locations**: Library West, Reitz Union, Student Recreation Center
   - **Local Restaurants**: Those already partnered with Bite Club
   - **Popular Student Areas**: Midtown, sorority/fraternity houses, apartment complexes

3. **Engagement Analysis**
   - **Comments Quality**: Genuine interactions vs bot comments
   - **Story Engagement**: High story views relative to posts
   - **Authenticity Markers**: Real student life content, not overly polished

#### Automated Tools (Optional)
- **HypeAuditor**: Influencer discovery and fake follower detection
- **Upfluence**: Campus-specific influencer search
- **AspireIQ**: Micro-influencer relationship management
- **Manual Alternative**: Create a spreadsheet tracking discovered influencers

### Compensation Structure

#### Tier 1: Trial Partnership (No commitment)
- **Offer**: $25-50 in Bite Club credits
- **Requirement**: Try the app, post one authentic review
- **Goal**: Test fit and gather authentic feedback
- **Duration**: One-time trial

#### Tier 2: Content Creator Partnership  
- **Offer**: $100-200/month in credits + performance bonuses
- **Requirements**: 2-4 posts per month, authentic usage documentation
- **Performance Bonus**: $25 per 1,000 app downloads from their content
- **Goal**: Regular content creation and app promotion
- **Duration**: 3-6 month agreements

#### Tier 3: Campus Ambassador
- **Offer**: $200-400/month in credits + cash payments for top performers
- **Requirements**: 4-6 posts per month, event participation, referral tracking
- **Additional Benefits**: Exclusive restaurant previews, VIP support
- **Goal**: Brand representation and community building
- **Duration**: Semester or academic year agreements

## Ambassador Program Structure

### Application Process

#### Step 1: Self-Application Form
```
Bite Club Campus Ambassador Application

Basic Info:
- Name, Instagram/TikTok handle, email
- Year in school, major
- Current followers (all platforms)
- Average engagement rate (if known)

Content Experience:
- How long have you been creating content?
- What type of content do you most enjoy creating?
- Share 3 of your best-performing posts (links)

Bite Club Alignment:
- How often do you currently order food delivery?
- What's your biggest challenge with current food delivery apps?
- Why are you interested in partnering with Bite Club?
- How would you authentically integrate Bite Club into your content?

Commitment:
- How many posts per month can you realistically commit to?
- Are you available for occasional campus events/meetups?
- What are your goals for this partnership?
```

#### Step 2: Trial Period (2 weeks)
- Provide $50 in credits
- Ask for 1-2 authentic trial posts
- Evaluate content quality, engagement, brand alignment
- Assess authenticity and audience response

#### Step 3: Program Onboarding
- Welcome package with branded materials
- Content guidelines and brand voice training
- Access to exclusive ambassador Discord/Slack
- Performance tracking setup

### Ambassador Responsibilities

#### Content Creation Requirements
- **Monthly Minimums**: 2-4 posts depending on tier
- **Content Mix**: 70% organic integration, 30% direct promotion
- **Authenticity Standard**: Must use app genuinely, no fake testimonials
- **Quality Baseline**: Professional phone photography, clear audio, good lighting

#### Community Building Activities
- **Campus Events**: Participate in Bite Club promotional events
- **Peer Referrals**: Encourage friends to try the app naturally
- **Feedback Collection**: Report user experience issues and suggestions
- **Market Intelligence**: Share competitor activities and campus food trends

#### Exclusive Benefits
- **Early Access**: New features, restaurant partnerships
- **VIP Support**: Direct line to customer service and technical team
- **Networking**: Connect with other campus ambassadors
- **Career Development**: LinkedIn recommendations, internship opportunities

### Performance Tracking & KPIs

#### Individual Ambassador Metrics
- **Reach**: Total impressions across all content
- **Engagement**: Likes, comments, shares, saves per post
- **Conversions**: App downloads tracked via personal referral codes
- **Quality Score**: Content authenticity and brand alignment rating
- **Retention**: User sign-up to first order conversion rate

#### Program-Wide Success Metrics
- **Brand Awareness**: Hashtag usage, organic mentions
- **Cost Per Acquisition**: Total program cost √∑ new users acquired
- **Ambassador Retention**: Percentage of ambassadors retained each semester
- **Content Performance**: Average engagement rate of ambassador content vs organic

### Content Guidelines for Ambassadors

#### Do's
- **Authentic Usage**: Only post when genuinely using the app
- **Personal Stories**: Share real experiences and savings calculations
- **Visual Consistency**: Use good lighting, clear images/videos
- **Disclosure**: Always include #BiteClubPartner or similar disclosure
- **Community Focus**: Tag friends, encourage group orders

#### Don'ts
- **Fake Testimonials**: Never exaggerate savings or experiences
- **Over-Promotion**: Balance promotional content with regular lifestyle posts
- **Competitor Bashing**: Don't directly attack other food delivery apps
- **Script Reading**: Avoid overly polished or obviously scripted content
- **Quantity Over Quality**: Better to post less with higher authenticity

## Influencer Event Strategy

### Monthly Campus Events

#### "Taste Test Tuesday"
- **Concept**: Side-by-side comparison events on campus
- **Setup**: Partner restaurant pop-ups with Bite Club ordering stations
- **Content**: Live reactions, taste tests, savings calculations
- **Influencer Role**: Document experience, interview other students

#### "Savings Challenge Week"
- **Concept**: Influencers document a week of food ordering
- **Challenge**: Use only Bite Club vs normal spending habits
- **Content**: Daily updates, running savings total, lifestyle impact
- **Prize**: Semester worth of food credits for top performers

#### "Restaurant Partner Spotlight"
- **Concept**: Behind-the-scenes access to partner restaurants
- **Content**: Kitchen tours, owner interviews, menu development
- **Exclusivity**: Only ambassador access to create FOMO
- **Output**: Long-form content about local business partnerships

### Semester Events

#### Back-to-School Ambassador Summit
- **Purpose**: Onboard new ambassadors, strategic planning
- **Activities**: Content planning, brand training, team building
- **Outcome**: Unified campaign launch for new semester

#### Finals Week Support Campaign
- **Concept**: Free delivery credits during finals week
- **Ambassador Role**: Study fuel content, stress-free ordering
- **Community Impact**: Support students during high-stress period

## Quality Control & Brand Safety

### Content Approval Process

#### Pre-Posting Review (For New Ambassadors)
- Submit content 24 hours before posting
- Quick approval/feedback cycle
- Ensures brand consistency during learning period

#### Spot-Check System (For Experienced Ambassadors)
- Random content auditing
- Monthly ambassador check-ins
- Performance and brand alignment reviews

### Brand Safety Measures
- **Crisis Communication Plan**: Guidelines for handling negative feedback
- **Ambassador Training**: What to do if app issues arise during filming
- **Escalation Process**: When to involve Bite Club team vs handle independently
- **Authenticity Monitoring**: Regular checks for fake engagement or testimonials
