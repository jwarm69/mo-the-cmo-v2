# Bite Club Channel Strategy & Weekly Content Framework

## On-Campus Tactics

### Tabling Strategy
- **Locations**: Student Union, Library West plaza, Turlington Plaza, Reitz Union, dorm entrances during move-in
- **Frequency**: 3-4 days/week during launch, 2x/week steady state
- **Props**: Spin-to-win prize wheel, photo booth with branded props, QR code banners for instant app download
- **Giveaways**: Free meal credits, stickers, branded water bottles
- **Key Tactic**: "Sign up in 30 seconds, get a free meal right now" — convert on the spot
- **Staff**: Campus ambassadors (Bite Club Campus Crew) wearing branded gear

### Ambassador Program ("Bite Club Campus Crew")
- **Structure**:
  - Crew Member (5 signups/month): Free meals + $50/month
  - Crew Leader (15 signups/month): Free meals + $150/month + swag
  - Campus Captain (30 signups/month): Free meals + $300/month + event budget
- **Activities**: Tabling, dorm storming, social media content, event hosting
- **Recruitment**: Target student org leaders, social media micro-influencers, Greek life members
- **Training**: 5-day onboarding covering brand voice, app demo, content creation basics

### Dorm Marketing
- **Move-In Day**: Door hangers, welcome bags with free meal coupon + QR code, branded water at dorm entrances
- **RA Partnerships**: RA referral incentives, floor representative program
- **Dorm Takeover Nights**: Monthly free dinner events in dorm common areas for first 50 residents
- **Flyer Placement**: Common rooms, laundry rooms, elevator lobbies (with permission)

### Greek Life Integration
- **Chapter Dinner Takeovers**: Monthly sponsored dinners at Greek houses
- **Philanthropy Event Partnerships**: Sponsor Greek philanthropy events with food
- **Group Plan Discounts**: Special pricing for houses ordering 20+ meals

## Partner Restaurant Amplification

### In-Restaurant Tactics
- **Tent Cards**: Table-top cards at partner restaurants: "Order ahead on Bite Club — skip the line"
- **Counter Stickers**: "Bite Club Partner" window/counter stickers for social proof
- **Staff Mentions**: Train partner staff to mention Bite Club for order-ahead pickup
- **Receipt Inserts**: "Next time, skip the line — order on Bite Club" cards in every bag
- **Menu Boards**: "Also available on Bite Club" callouts on physical menus

### Co-Marketing
- **Joint social media posts**: Partner restaurants share Bite Club content with their followers
- **Exclusive items**: "Bite Club only" menu items or combos at select partners
- **Restaurant content**: Behind-the-scenes kitchen tours, chef features, ingredient stories

## Weekly Content Framework (Mon-Sun Schedule)

### Monday — "Money Monday"
- **Theme**: Cost savings, budget content, financial motivation
- **Post**: Cost comparison content or savings reveal
- **Email**: Weekly savings recap for active users
- **Series**: Order With Me (showing price comparisons)
- **Copy Angle**: "New week, smarter spending"

### Tuesday — "Testimonial Tuesday"
- **Theme**: Social proof, student stories, reviews
- **Post**: Student testimonial video or UGC reshare
- **Series**: Between Classes Meals
- **Copy Angle**: Real students, real experiences

### Wednesday — "What's Cooking Wednesday"
- **Theme**: Restaurant content, food discovery, menu spotlights
- **Post**: Restaurant partner spotlight or menu feature
- **Series**: Hidden Gem Spotlight OR Best ___ On
- **Copy Angle**: Discover something new

### Thursday — "Throwback Thursday" / Peak Engagement
- **Theme**: Campus culture, nostalgia, high-engagement content
- **Post**: Campus culture content, memes, relatable moments
- **Series**: $X Challenge OR Closest Meals To
- **Copy Angle**: Peak engagement day — post strongest content

### Friday — "Feature Friday"
- **Theme**: App features, tips, weekend prep
- **Post**: How-to content or app feature tutorial
- **Series**: Swipe Stories
- **Copy Angle**: Set up your weekend eating

### Saturday — "Social Saturday"
- **Theme**: Weekend lifestyle, group orders, game day content
- **Post**: Lifestyle/social content, game day food prep
- **Series**: Line vs No Line OR How It Works
- **Copy Angle**: Weekend vibes, social dining

### Sunday — "Setup Sunday"
- **Theme**: Week-ahead planning, new drops, meal prep alternative
- **Post**: Promo drops, new restaurant announcements, week preview
- **Series**: Promo Drop (if applicable)
- **Copy Angle**: Plan your week, skip the meal prep

## Still/Infographic Content Types

### Type A: Receipt Comparison
- Side-by-side receipts: DoorDash vs Bite Club for the same order
- Large, clear price difference highlighted
- Monthly extrapolation: "× 20 orders = $173/month saved"

### Type B: Savings Calculator Graphic
- Visual calculator showing user inputs: "If you order X times/week..."
- DoorDash total vs Bite Club total
- Big savings number highlighted with aspirational spend: "That's a spring break fund"

### Type C: Menu Spotlight Card
- Beautiful food photo with restaurant name
- Price on Bite Club vs delivery app price
- "Available now" CTA with QR code

### Type D: Student Spotlight Quote Card
- Student photo (or illustrated avatar)
- Pull quote: "I saved $400 last semester switching to Bite Club"
- Name, year, major
- Bite Club branded frame

### Type E: "By the Numbers" Infographic
- Key stats in a visually engaging layout
- Examples: "2,847 students. $340K saved. 15,000+ meals ordered."
- Monthly or semester recaps

### Type F: "How It Works" Carousel
- 3-slide Instagram carousel
- Slide 1: The problem (delivery fees, long waits)
- Slide 2: The solution (Bite Club in 3 steps)
- Slide 3: The offer (3 free meals, link in bio)

### Type G: Campus Map Graphic
- Illustrated map of campus with Bite Club partner restaurants marked
- Walking times from key buildings
- "Your next meal is __ minutes away"

### Type H: Weekly Menu Preview
- Grid of 6-8 featured meals available this week
- Prices shown for each
- "This week on Bite Club" header

### Type I: Game Day Graphic
- UF game day themed design
- "Fuel up before the game" or "Post-game meals, zero fees"
- Featured game day meal deals

### Type J: Seasonal Campaign Graphic
- Themed for specific events: Move-In Week, Finals, Homecoming, Spring Break
- Event-specific offer
- Branded consistently but with seasonal flair

## Gainesville-Specific Campaign Hooks

### Move-In Week (August)
- "Move-In Day Rescue" tabling with free water and snacks at dorms
- "Freshman Starter Pack" — first 3 meals free for new students
- Parent-targeted messaging: "Make sure your kid actually eats well"
- Door hangers on every dorm room: "Your first meal is on us"

### Game Days (Football Season)
- Pre-game meal deals: "Fuel up before kickoff"
- Post-game food: "Skip the post-game line, eat now"
- Tailgate partnerships: feed your crew for less
- Game day social content series

### Finals Week (December & April)
- "Study Fuel Stations" at Library West — free coffee and snacks
- "Midnight Breakfast" delivery to study spaces
- "Brain Food" meal deals — discounted orders during finals
- Extended hours and last-minute ordering

### Homecoming (October)
- Alumni + current student combo deals
- "Welcome Back" themed content
- Restaurant partner specials

### Spring Break (March)
- "Save for Spring Break" messaging — show cumulative savings
- "Pre-break meal deals" — use credits before you leave
- Return messaging: "Welcome back, your Bite Club is waiting"

### New Semester (January)
- "New Year, Smarter Eating" campaign
- Re-engagement emails for dormant users
- Welcome offers for new transfer students
- "Resolution" themed content: eat better, spend less

## Posting Schedule by Platform

### TikTok
- **Frequency**: 1-2x daily
- **Best Times (EST)**: 12:00 PM, 7:00 PM, 9:00 PM
- **Best Days**: Tuesday-Thursday
- **Content Focus**: Short-form video series, trending sounds, authentic style

### Instagram Reels
- **Frequency**: 1x daily
- **Best Times (EST)**: 11:00 AM, 2:00 PM, 5:00 PM
- **Best Days**: Wednesday-Friday
- **Content Focus**: Slightly more polished, carousel posts, Stories for daily engagement

### YouTube Shorts
- **Frequency**: 3-4x/week
- **Best Times (EST)**: 3:00 PM, 8:00 PM
- **Best Days**: Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday
- **Content Focus**: Slightly longer format, educational/how-to content
