# Bite Club Growth Marketing Content Strategy

## Brand Voice & Messaging Framework

### Core Brand Voice
- **Tone**: Authentic, relatable, money-conscious, student-friendly
- **Personality**: Your savvy roommate who always knows the best deals
- **Voice**: Conversational, not corporate. "Your wallet will thank you" not "Optimize your dining expenditure"

### Key Messaging Pillars

#### 1. Financial Savings
- **Primary Message**: "Skip the fees, keep the money"
- **Supporting Points**:
  - Zero service charges vs $8+ DoorDash fees
  - Credits never expire vs $450+ lost dining hall credits annually
  - Same great food at actual menu prices

#### 2. Convenience & Time Savings  
- **Primary Message**: "Skip the lines, skip the wait"
- **Supporting Points**:
  - Order ahead pickup
  - No delivery waiting times
  - Campus-optimized locations

#### 3. Student Lifestyle Integration
- **Primary Message**: "Made for students, by students"
- **Supporting Points**:
  - Understand college budget constraints
  - Campus-focused restaurant partnerships
  - Flexible credit system that works with student schedules

### Content Pillars (What We Talk About)

#### Pillar 1: Cost Comparison Content (35% of posts)
**Purpose**: Drive home the savings message with concrete examples
**Content Types**:
- Side-by-side receipt comparisons
- Monthly spending breakdowns
- "What I bought with money I saved" reveals
- Calculator/math-based content

#### Pillar 2: Student Success Stories (25% of posts)
**Purpose**: Social proof and relatability
**Content Types**:
- User testimonials and reviews
- "Day in my life using Bite Club" videos
- Before/after lifestyle content
- Student interviews

#### Pillar 3: Restaurant & Food Content (20% of posts)
**Purpose**: Showcase variety and quality
**Content Types**:
- Restaurant partner spotlights
- Behind-the-scenes kitchen tours
- Menu highlights and recommendations
- Food photography and styling

#### Pillar 4: Campus Culture & Lifestyle (15% of posts)
**Purpose**: Build community and engagement
**Content Types**:
- UF campus references and inside jokes
- Study session fuel recommendations
- Game day food prep
- Seasonal campus events tie-ins

#### Pillar 5: Educational/How-To Content (5% of posts)
**Purpose**: App adoption and feature education
**Content Types**:
- App tutorial videos
- "How to maximize your credits" tips
- Feature announcements
- FAQ addressing

## Visual Identity Guidelines

### Color Palette
- Primary: Bite Club brand colors (maintain consistency with existing app)
- Secondary: Warm, food-focused colors (oranges, reds, yellows)
- Accent: UF blue and orange when appropriate for campus content

### Typography
- Headers: Bold, easy-to-read sans-serif fonts
- Body text: Clean, mobile-optimized readability
- Overlays: High contrast for video text overlays

### Photography Style
- Authentic, unfiltered student life moments
- Natural lighting when possible
- Focus on real people, real food, real savings
- Avoid overly polished or stock photo aesthetics

### Video Style
- Vertical format optimized (9:16 aspect ratio)
- Quick cuts and dynamic pacing
- Text overlays for key information
- Trending audio when appropriate

## Content Quality Standards

### Authenticity Requirements
- Real students, real experiences, real savings
- No scripted testimonials - genuine reactions only
- Actual app screenshots and receipts
- Honest about any limitations or challenges

### Production Quality
- Clear audio (use external mics when needed)
- Steady footage (tripods/stabilizers)
- Good lighting (natural or simple ring lights)
- Clean editing with consistent style

### Compliance & Legal
- Obtain proper permissions for student features
- Include necessary disclaimers for savings claims
- Respect restaurant partner branding guidelines
- Follow platform-specific content policies

## Content Approval Process

### Tier 1: Quick Approval (Same Day)
- Basic promotional content using established templates
- Restaurant highlights and menu features
- Simple comparison posts

### Tier 2: Standard Approval (2-3 Days)
- Student testimonials and success stories
- New content format experiments
- Collaborative content with influencers

### Tier 3: Full Review (1 Week)
- Campaigns involving specific savings claims
- Partnership announcements
- Crisis response or sensitive topics

## Success Metrics for Content Strategy

### Engagement Metrics
- Like rate: Target 5-8% of views
- Comment rate: Target 2-3% of views
- Share/save rate: Target 3-5% of views
- Completion rate: Target 70%+ for video content

### Conversion Metrics
- Click-through rate to app: Target 3-5%
- App downloads from social: Track by UTM codes
- Sign-up conversion: Target 25% of clicks
- First order completion: Target 60% of sign-ups

### Brand Awareness Metrics
- Hashtag usage and user-generated content
- Brand mention sentiment analysis
- Campus brand recognition surveys
- Organic search volume for "Bite Club UF"
