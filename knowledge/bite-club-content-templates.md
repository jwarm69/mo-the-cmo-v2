## Quick Production Tips

### Essential Equipment (Budget-Friendly)
- **Phone with good camera** (iPhone 12+ or equivalent Android)
- **Small tripod or phone mount** ($15-25)
- **Ring light or natural lighting** (window lighting works great)
- **External microphone** (optional but recommended for better audio)

### Editing Apps Recommendations
- **CapCut** (free, powerful, trending effects)
- **InShot** (simple, good for beginners)
- **Adobe Premiere Rush** (more advanced features)
- **Native platform editors** (TikTok and Instagram built-in)

### Trending Audio Strategy
1. **Daily trending check** - Check TikTok Creative Center for trending sounds
2. **Audio adaptation** - Use trending audio but adapt it to Bite Club context  
3. **Original audio** - Create quotable moments that could become trends
4. **Sound quality** - Always prioritize clear audio over visual effects

### Content Batch Creation Tips
1. **Film multiple templates in one session**
2. **Prepare outfit changes** for variety
3. **Use different locations** on campus
4. **Create content bank** for consistent posting
5. **Document real experiences** - always have phone ready for authentic moments
